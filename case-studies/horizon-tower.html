Horizon Tower

Mixed-Use Development • Miami, Florida • 2024

Project Overview

A vertical community defining Miami's evolving skyline

Horizon Tower represents a new paradigm in luxury high-rise living, combining premium residential units with sophisticated commercial spaces and world-class amenities. This 40-story landmark building redefines the relationship between private luxury and community living, creating a vertical neighborhood that captures the dynamic spirit of modern Miami.

The project responds to Miami's unique climate and cultural context while addressing contemporary challenges of urban density, environmental resilience, and luxury market expectations. Through innovative design strategies and cutting-edge technology, Horizon Tower sets new standards for high-rise development in subtropical environments.

Design Challenge

Creating luxury living that withstands Miami's climate extremes

The development required a tower that could command premium pricing in Miami's competitive luxury market while meeting stringent hurricane resistance standards and flood resilience requirements. The building needed to accommodate diverse program requirements including luxury residences, premium office space, retail, and extensive amenities.

Environmental challenges included designing for Category 5 hurricane winds, rising sea levels, and extreme heat while maintaining floor-to-ceiling glazing and outdoor amenities that luxury buyers expect. The project also needed to achieve LEED certification and incorporate advanced building systems for energy efficiency.

Market Success

Performance metrics and market reception

Horizon Tower achieved 95% pre-sales before construction completion, with units commanding record prices for the Miami market. The building's innovative approach to hurricane resilience and luxury amenities attracted international buyers and established new benchmarks for high-rise development in the region.

The project received LEED Gold certification and recognition from the Council on Tall Buildings and Urban Habitat for its integration of resilience and luxury. The building has weathered multiple hurricane seasons without damage, validating the enhanced structural design strategies.
