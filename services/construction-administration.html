Construction Administration

Ensuring design integrity through comprehensive project oversight

Professional Project Oversight

Protecting your investment through expert construction administration

Our construction administration services bridge the crucial gap between design intent and built reality, ensuring that your architectural vision is realized with precision and quality. We provide comprehensive oversight throughout the construction process, from groundbreaking to final occupancy, protecting both the client's interests and the integrity of the design.

Through regular site visits, detailed progress reviews, and proactive problem-solving, our team coordinates closely with contractors, consultants, and clients to maintain project schedules, budgets, and quality standards while addressing challenges that inevitably arise during construction.

Administration Process

A systematic approach to construction oversight and quality control

01

Pre-Construction

Contractor selection assistance, contract review, and pre-construction meetings to establish protocols.

02

Regular Site Visits

Scheduled site inspections to monitor progress, quality, and compliance with construction documents.

03

Progress Review

Monthly progress evaluations, payment certifications, and schedule coordination with all project stakeholders.

04

Issue Resolution

Proactive identification and resolution of construction issues, change order evaluation, and design clarifications.

05

Project Closeout

Final inspections, punch list management, warranty coordination, and project documentation completion.
